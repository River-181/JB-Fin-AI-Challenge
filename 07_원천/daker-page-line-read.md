## Mission Reading

010. The challenge asks teams to build an AI Agent service connected to real financial business.

011. The page emphasizes services that understand customer situations, make judgments, and take action.

012. Teams select one topic and implement an MVP.

013. For the online preliminary, teams submit an MVP proposal and function specification.

014. Demo video is optional, but it can add up to 5 bonus points.

015. The top 13 teams are selected for the final stage through vote and judge evaluation.

## Submission Reading

018. Optional file: demo video within 5 minutes and 100 MB.

019. The MVP proposal should keep the provided 7-section structure.

020. The function specification is recommended around 3 pages.

021. Final submission cannot be modified after submission.

022. Additional GitHub, code, and demo materials can be requested.

## Agent Requirements

023. Judgment: interpret input, situation, and data, then derive status, risk, or priority.

024. Action: choose the next action such as asking, referencing, drafting, notifying, summarizing, or scheduling.

025. Verification and improvement: check results, then retry, stop, or improve.

026. The MVP must show more than a static analysis report. It should demonstrate an operating loop.
